About Us
We are an established medical billing company with a team of professional that are ready to take the worries and frustrations of medical billing off of your hands and allow you more time to do what you love, which is take care of you patients.

Our team members are all well versed in the IC9 coding and have all been trained and certified in the new ICD10 billing codes. We are up to date with all the HIPPA compliance laws and are constantly updating ourselves, we strive to stay up to date with what is going on in the world of Medical Billing to keep our custumers up to date as well, we undestand as medical profesional you do not have the time to keep up to speed with all the changes that are going on, that is where we come in. Doctors have too many other things to worry about, besides accurate medical billing.
